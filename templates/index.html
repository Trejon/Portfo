About

Hi there! I'm Trejon from Denver, Colorado. I'm an avid learner who attended the Full-Stack Software Engineering program at Flatiron School. I've been programming for over 8 years. I have a lot of experience with JavaScript, Ruby and Python. I've also worked extensively with frameworks and libraries such as Rails, Sinatra, React, Redux, Angular, Svelte and more. In my spare time, I enjoy digging into machine learning with Python and experimenting with IoT, especially focusing on Raspberry Pi projects.

I completed two years of coursework in finance at Metropolitan State University of Denver. While attending the university and working full time as a bank teller, my work was drastically changed by new technology. A new banking system rolled out, changing my daily responsibilities as well as the entire customer experience. The impact of the new system and efficiency it provided, rekindled my curiosity and passion for the power of technology.

I spent the next year taking online courses to learn more about different technologies and trying to better understand the industry, ultimately leading me to Flatiron School. Aside from my core curriculum, I developed a deeper passion for problem solving and programmatic thinking, and improved my expertise in algorithms and data structures. Through the program and many projects I've built, my childhood hobby quickly turned into an adult obsession. I'm excited to continue expanding my knowledge and developing my skill set in the dynamic world of tech.
